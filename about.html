About Us

Our company was founded in 1996 in Çankırı under the name USTALAR MÜHENDİSLİK İNŞAAT TAAHÜT ÇELİK KONSTRÜKSİYON TİCARET VE SANAYİ LTD. ŞTİ.
Later, in 2018, it was renamed MİRALP ÇELİK YAPI PROJE İNŞAAT İTHALAT İHRACAT SANAYİ VE TİCARET LTD. ŞTİ.
We operate in the field of design and construction of industrial buildings.
All types of industrial building projects are prepared and licensed in accordance with current regulations and specifications.
We have a steel construction workshop with an annual steel manufacturing and assembly capacity of 2,800 tons.
Between 2019 and 2024, our company exported a total of 1,000 tons of steel to Romania and Iraq.
We employ a total of 47 staff, including 7 technical personnel, 24 manufacturing personnel, and 16 assembly personnel.

Our Vision

To be a leading brand in Turkey and internationally with innovative, reliable, and sustainable steel structure solutions in our sector. We aim to lead the development of industrial buildings by offering high engineering standards, quality, and economical projects to our customers.

Our Mission

In the design and construction of industrial buildings, to produce safe and durable steel structures tailored to customer needs and in compliance with regulations. By closely following technological developments, we aim to add value to the sector with a continuous improvement and high-quality approach, prioritizing the development of our employees and customer satisfaction.
